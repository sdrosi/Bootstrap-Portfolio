About Me

When I envision a brighter society, words like connectivity, shared experience, and inclusivity come to mind. We are in a unique position today to use technology to bridge the gaps that exist within our communities. Through shared knowledge and resources, we have the opportunity to create systemic change. When I think about the future, it is a place where all persons have an equal opportunity to thrive, regardless of their race, gender, ethnicity, birth place, experiences, history, or ability. To achieve this goal, we must work together to identify where improvements can be made and where services are needed. I am excited to be a piece of this puzzle. Will you join me?
